The Mamba Mentality

The "mamba mentality" is the ideology created by Kobe Bryant, a star basketbll player,

who is best known for having the capabilities to grow every day and spend hours analyzing

his opponents before a match. Managing to play through a broken finger on his shooting arms

and the tearing of his Achilles tendon(a severe injury permanently affecting the body's movement),

he managed to keep his crumbling body together with will power, brute strength, and the mentality he carried

for over 20 years
